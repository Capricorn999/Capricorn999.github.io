About Me

I am currently a Master of Computer Science student at the University of Macau. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Macau, with an Artificial Intelligence concentration. My research interests include multimodal learning, AI security and trustworthy AI, biometric information protection, computer vision, and related areas.

My recent research explores age progression for missing children, facial dynamics for video-based kinship verification, and training-free full-hand de-identification for biometric privacy.

Publications

Manuscripts and Research Projects

  • DynamicKin: Learning Facial Dynamics via Optical Flow for Video-Based Kinship Verification, Yifan Lyu, Bob Zhang*, Under review for IJCB 2026.
    Proposes a video-based kinship verification framework that combines facial appearance features with optical-flow-based facial motion cues, together with the Smiling Kinship Videos dataset.
  • A Self-Semantic-Structural-Guided Approach for Training-Free Full-Hand De-Identification, Licheng Yan, Yifan Lyu, Weiliang Huang, Bob Zhang*, Andrew Beng Jin Teoh, Lu Leng, Shuyi Li, Shuping Zhao, Under review for TIFS.
    Develops a diffusion-based inpainting framework for hand biometric privacy, preserving image realism and background consistency across datasets and recognition systems.
  • FedSIDA: Federated Spectral-Invariant Discriminative Alignment for Multispectral Palmprint Recognition with Incomplete Modalities, Yuqi Wang†, Shenghui Huang†, Yifan Lyu, Licheng Yan, Jintao Wang, Shuyi Li, Bob Zhang*, Under review for TIFS.
    Proposes a federated palmprint recognition framework for privacy-preserving multispectral biometrics under identity-disjoint clients, spectral heterogeneity, and missing modalities. The method shares anonymized spectral discriminative structures rather than identity semantics, enabling robust cross-spectral alignment without shared identities or paired cross-spectral samples.
  • Research on Age Progression of Missing Children, Supervisor: Prof. Yibo Bob Zhang.
    Progressed children's faces to a desired age domain using four CycleGANs with local and global transformations. The model was trained on UTK-Face and YLFW datasets using NVIDIA A100 GPU resources on the University of Macau HPCC. Ranked 3rd in Best Final Year Projects, CIS 2023/2024.

Selected Projects

  • Traffic Sign Classification, Python, Jupyter Notebook, Scikit-learn. Used SVM, Random Forest, and MLP models with CLAHE and Standard Scaler preprocessing on the German Traffic Sign Benchmark dataset. F1 score: 97%.
  • Named Entity Recognition, Python. Implemented NER with a Maximum Entropy Markov Model, including n-grams, POS-tagging, and other feature experiments. Built an online Flask application. F1 score: 95%.
  • Pixel-wise Contour Detection, MATLAB. Implemented the Moore Algorithm to extract border pixels from binary images and applied Jacob's Rule to improve performance.
  • King of Karaoke, Java, Android Studio. Built Android application features including authentication, a quiz template, music playback, score upload, and result display using Auth0, JSONBin, and imageio APIs.
