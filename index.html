Final Year Project · Supervisor: Prof. Yibo Bob Zhang

Research on Age Progression of Missing Children

Built an age progression approach for children's faces using four CycleGAN models that combine local and global transformations to preserve identity while transferring appearance across age domains.

Trained on UTK-Face and YLFW datasets using NVIDIA A100 Tensor Core GPU resources on the University of Macau HPCC.

Ranked 3rd in Best Final Year Projects, CIS 2023/2024
Under review · IJCB 2026

DynamicKin: Learning Facial Dynamics via Optical Flow for Video-Based Kinship Verification

Proposed DynamicKin, a video-based kinship verification framework combining facial appearance features with optical-flow-based facial motion cues.

Introduced the Smiling Kinship Videos dataset to support future work in dynamic kinship verification.

Yifan Lyu, Bob Zhang*
Under review · TIFS

A Self-Semantic-Structural-Guided Approach for Training-Free Full-Hand De-Identification

Developed a diffusion-based inpainting framework that uses semantic and structural guidance to protect hand biometric privacy while preserving image realism and background consistency.

The method removes identity-related hand features and maintains usability across multiple datasets and recognition systems.
